For those of you who want to know what the difference is please read this: ("The key difference between baby dedication and infant baptism lies in their traditions and meanings. Baby dedication, common in Evangelical and non-denominational churches, is a ceremony where parents publicly commit to raising their child according to Christian values, with the support of the church community. It involves no water and has no connection to salvation, focusing instead on the child's future faith journey. In contrast, infant baptism, practiced in Catholic, Orthodox, and some Protestant traditions, is a sacrament that uses water to symbolize spiritual cleansing and marks the child's initiation into the faith community. Some traditions view baptism as necessary for salvation or as the beginning of a life of grace.
